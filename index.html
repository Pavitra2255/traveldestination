Must Visit Temples in India!

Quick Navigation

Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh

The Venkateswara Temple (Tirupati Balaji) in Andhra Pradesh is one of the world's richest and most-visited Hindu pilgrimage sites, dedicated to Lord Venkateswara

Kasi, Uttar Pradesh

The Kashi Vishwanath Temple, located in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, is one of India's most revered Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva, housing one of the twelve Jyotirlingas

Jagannath Temple, Odisha

The Jagannath Temple in Puri, Odisha, is a major 12th-century Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Jagannath (a form of Vishnu), along with his siblings Balabhadra and Subhadra

Meenakshi Temple, Tamil Nadu

The Meenakshi Amman Temple is a historic and massive Hindu temple complex located in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India, dedicated to Meenakshi (a form of Parvati) and her consort, Sundareswarar (Shiva).
